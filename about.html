About Me

A very normal person with a very normal life

Welcome to my corner of the internet!

My name is Dat Hoang Tran. I'm a last year student at VNU University of Science.

I live a boring life—or at least I think so. Day after day, I study and work just like countless others around the world. I long to find a way out, an escape from this life.

I enjoy cooking—at least I think I do. There's something truly comforting about preparing ingredients and savoring the result of my own creation. That said, I cook mainly for myself, so I honestly don't care much about what others think of the dishes I make.

I often experiment with unconventional methods, mixing different ingredients to create unique dishes that don't follow any standard recipe. My cooking tends to focus on the freshness and natural flavors of the ingredients, with minimal use of pre-made seasonings.

I simply share what I know, the things I discover along the journey of finding myself, with the hope that someday, someone will pull me out into the world and lead me to things I could never reach on my own.
